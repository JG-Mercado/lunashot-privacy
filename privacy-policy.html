Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

LunaShot is a camera app for long exposure and night photography. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

LunaShot does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or registration is required to use the app.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or usage tracking is in place.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers by the app itself.
  • Photos you capture are saved directly to your device's Photos library and never leave your device through this app.

In-App Purchases

LunaShot offers a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not handle payment information directly. When you make a purchase:

  • Payment is processed by Apple. Apple's Privacy Policy governs how your payment data is handled.
  • We receive only a confirmation that a purchase was made, with no personal or financial details.
  • Purchase status is stored locally on your device via Apple's StoreKit framework.

To restore a previous purchase, you can use the "Restore Purchase" option in the app. This also goes through Apple and does not involve us directly.

Camera and Photos

LunaShot requires access to your camera and photo library to function. This access is used solely to capture images and save them to your device. No images or camera data are transmitted anywhere.

Motion Data

The app uses your device's motion sensors to display a horizon level indicator. This data is processed locally and is never stored or transmitted.

Children's Privacy

LunaShot does not knowingly collect any information from children under 16. Since we collect no personal data at all, no special handling is required.

Changes to This Policy

If the app ever introduces features that change how data is handled, this policy will be updated and the date at the top will reflect that change.
